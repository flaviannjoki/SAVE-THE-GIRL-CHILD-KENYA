Why make a donation and how?

Save the girl child Kenya is an initiative that educates the girl child in Kenya about menstrual physiology and also looks for ways to provide sanitary resources to the needy girl child in Kenya who has no access to materials such as sanitary pads or menstrual cups for one reason or another.

Save the girl child Kenya is dependant on donations from well-wishers who see the need to support this good cause.We receive physical goods such as sanitary pads and menstrual cups, we also receive financial aid that helps us acquire these sanitary reources which are later distributed to groups that need them the most, including but not limited to:
  1. Female street children
  2. Children homes
  3. Girls in deeply remote areas
  4. Deserving primary and secondary schools
